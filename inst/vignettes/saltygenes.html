Salty Genes

This vignette from the R package canprot version 0.2.0 shows compositional metrics and phylostrata for proteins coded by genes that are differentially expressed in hyperosmotic compared to control conditions. Abbreviations:

  • ZC – carbon oxidation state; nH2O – stoichiometric hydration state; nO2 – stoichiometric oxidation state.
  • nAA – protein length.
  • ndown – number of down-regulated proteins; nup – number of up-regulated proteins.

Stoichiometric values are calculated using basis species (rQEC derivation) or amino acid biosynthetic reactions (Dick et al., 2020; Dick, 2020).

datasets <- pdat_saltygenes(2020)

Dashed contour lines in the plots outline the 50% credible region for highest probability density.

In the table, values of ΔZC, ΔnH2O, ΔnO2 and ΔGRAVY are multiplied by 1000, values of ΔpI are multiplied by 100, and negative values are shown in bold.
