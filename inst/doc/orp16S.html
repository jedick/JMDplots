Geochemical influence on protein evolution (2023)

This vignette runs the code to make the plots from the following paper:

Dick JM, Meng D. 2023. Community reference proteomes suggest widespread influence of geochemistry on bacterial protein evolution. Submitted manuscript.

Preprint available at bioRxiv. doi: 10.1101/2021.10.12.464155

This vignette was compiled on 2023-02-13 with JMDplots 1.2.17 and chem16S 0.1.3.
