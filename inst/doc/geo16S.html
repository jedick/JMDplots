Chemical links between redox gradients and estimated community proteomes (2022)

This vignette runs the code to make the plots from the following paper first published by Springer Nature:

Dick JM, Tan J. 2022. Chemical links between redox conditions and estimated community proteomes from 16S rRNA and reference protein sequences. Microbial Ecology. doi: 10.1007/s00248-022-01988-9

Use this link for full-text access to a view-only version of the paper: https://rdcu.be/cMCDa. A preprint of the paper is available on bioRxiv at doi: 10.1101/2021.05.31.446500.

This vignette was compiled on 2022-09-23 with JMDplots 1.2.14.
