Thermodynamics in evolution and development (2021)

This vignette runs the code to make the plots from the following paper:

Dick JM. 2021. A thermodynamic model for water activity and redox potential in evolution and development. Submitted manuscript.

Preprint available at:

Dick JM. 2021. Redox potential linked to water loss from proteins in evolution and development. bioRxiv. doi: 10.1101/2021.01.29.428804

This vignette was compiled on 2021-07-15 with JMDplots 1.2.7, CHNOSZ 1.4.1, and canprot 1.1.1.

library(JMDplots)

To reduce running time, the plots in this vignette are made with 99 bootstrap replicates. To reproduce the plots in the paper, the boot.R argument should be changed to 999.

Chemical analysis of Trigos and Liebeskind datasets (Figure 1)

Data sources: Phylostrata are from Trigos et al. (2017). Consensus gene ages are from Liebeskind et al. (2016).
