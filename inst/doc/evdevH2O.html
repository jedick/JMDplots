This vignette contains plots from the paper by Dick (2021). This vignette was compiled on 2021-01-29 with JMDplots 1.2.5 and CHNOSZ 1.4.0-3.

Phylostrata are from Trigos et al. (2017). Consensus gene ages are from Liebeskind et al. (2016).
