Thermodynamic model for water activity and redox potential in evolution and development (2021)

This vignette runs the code to make the plots from the following paper:

Dick JM. 2021. A thermodynamic model for water activity and redox potential in evolution and development. Submitted manuscript.

Preprint available at:

Dick JM. 2021. Redox potential linked to water loss from proteins in evolution and development. bioRxiv. doi: 10.1101/2021.01.29.428804

This vignette was compiled on 2021-11-07 with JMDplots 1.2.9, CHNOSZ 1.4.1, and canprot 1.1.1.

library(JMDplots)

To reduce running time, the plots in this vignette are made with 99 bootstrap replicates. To reproduce the plots in the paper, the boot.R argument should be changed to 999.

The plots in the paper were made on 2021-07-15 with JMDplots 1.2.7. After that time, the following minor changes were made to the plots in this vignette:

  • 2021-07-18. The version of Figure 1 in the paper was made by counting unique UniProt IDs among all phylostrata; duplicates were removed after the first occurrence. This resulted in the removal of a relatively small number (175) of phylostrata assignments. The plots have been updated in this vignette to use counts for all genes with a UniProt mapping (including duplicates).

  • 2021-08-24 Lines showing the cumulative mean were removed from Figure 1. These lines are not discussed in the text.
