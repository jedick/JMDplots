Energy release in protein synthesis (2021)

This vignette runs the code to make the plots from the following paper:

Dick JM, Shock EL. 2021. The release of energy during protein synthesis at submarine hydrothermal ecosystems. Submitted manuscript.

This vignette was compiled on 2021-09-02 with JMDplots 1.2.7-18 and CHNOSZ 1.4.1.

library(JMDplots)

Calculations shown in this and other figures use activities of CH4, CO2, NH4+, H2S, H2, and H+ from the mixing models of Shock and Canovas (2010).
